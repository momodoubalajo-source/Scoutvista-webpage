CloudVista: your world, minus the noise.

CloudVista learns the markets, people and signals that matter to you – then comes back with a short list of moves worth acting on, every week.

Set up your personal AI See where it helps

No dashboards to babysit. No feed to doom-scroll. Just a personal AI that quietly learns your pattern.

Each week, CloudVista brings you…

  • 3–7 concrete moves where your insight or relationships actually matter.
  • A shortlist of accounts, people or contracts to nudge – not 200 “potential leads”.
  • A simple narrative of why these surfaced, based on how you’ve acted before.

You stay close to conversations and decisions. CloudVista handles the monitoring and pattern-spotting in the background.

What CloudVista does for you

Knows your world

You tell it the markets, accounts and people you care about. CloudVista ignores everything else.

Learns your pattern

You like, dismiss and act on signals. Over time it learns what “real opportunity” looks like in your work.

Reduces your noise

Instead of an alert firehose, you get a ranked shortlist of things worth a call, email or bid this week.

How CloudVista serves different worlds

Same engine, different uses. CloudVista adapts to the way you win – whether that’s retained searches, investment decisions or public sector contracts.

Executive search & recruitment

For when being early, relevant and non-annoying is the whole game.

  • Flags leadership changes, new units and sudden exits inside your target organisations.
  • Spots “headcount shock” moments that are likely to lead to retained searches.
  • Surfaces warm angles where you already know someone in the decision chain.

Output: a small set of organisations and people to approach with a point of view, not a generic pitch.

Finance & investment

For investors and analysts who care more about inflection points than headlines.

  • Tracks leadership, hiring and product shifts across the companies on your watchlist.
  • Highlights patterns that often precede raises, write-downs or exits in your universe.
  • Learns which combinations of signals matched past deals you actually acted on.

Output: a handful of moves and names worth a deeper look or a call to your network this week.

Government & public sector bids

For teams who need to see the work before it hits the tender portal.

  • Monitors the specific departments, agencies and programmes you sell into.
  • Spots pilots, trials and policy shifts that often grow into longer-term contracts.
  • Connects upcoming opportunities to the suppliers, partners and frameworks you sit on.

Output: early context and suggested relationships to nurture now, before the ITT drops.
