CloudVista learns the markets, people and signals that matter to you – then comes back with a short list of moves worth acting on, every week.

Set up your personal AI scout See where it helps

No dashboards to babysit. No feed to doom-scroll. Just a personal AI scout that quietly learns your pattern and tells you what matters.

Each week, your AI scout brings you…

  • 5–10 concrete moves where your insight or relationships actually matter.
  • A shortlist of accounts, contacts or contracts to nudge – not 200 “potential leads”.
  • A simple narrative of why these surfaced, based on how you’ve acted before.

You stay close to conversations and decisions. Your personal AI scout handles the monitoring and pattern-spotting in the background.

What CloudVista does for you

Understands your world

You give it the markets, organisations and network you actually care about. Your personal AI scout ignores everything outside that universe.

Learns your network

You upload the accounts and contacts you work with. Over time, CloudVista learns where your relationships are strongest and where they unlock access.

Focuses you on what matters

Instead of an alert firehose, you get a ranked shortlist of opportunities that look like the ones you actually convert.
